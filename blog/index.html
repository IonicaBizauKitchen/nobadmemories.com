Delete: A Design History of Computer Vapourware

Anyone interested in the history of computer design would do well to check out Paul Atkinson's Delete: A Design History of Computer Vapourware. Delete is packed with numerous full-color images—many of them original to this text—depicting computer hardware that was announced but never commercially produced. Atkinson's interviews with designers and executives close to these unrealized products are equally elucidating. If you'd like to learn more about Delete, take a look at my in-depth review, featured in the latest issue of Design and Culture. The PDF is available for free online.
